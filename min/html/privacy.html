Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important. This site is designed to be as minimal as possible.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect personal information. We do not have a registration system, and we do not track your identity.

2. Server Logs

Like most web servers, we log your IP address, browser type, and the time of your visit. This information is used strictly for security, rate limiting, and debugging server errors.

3. Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or share any logged data with third parties. No advertising scripts or third-party trackers are loaded on this site.
